*   **Logic:**

    *   Capturing metadata (source name, timestamp, original URL).

## Slide 6: The NLP Engine (Preprocessing)
*   **Techniques used:**
    *   **Lowercasing:** Removing case sensitivity.
    *   **Tokenization:** Breaking text into semantic units.
    *   **Stemming:** Reducing words to their linguistic roots (Porter Stemmer).
    *   **Filter:** Removing high-frequency "Stop Words" to focus on meaning.

---

## Slide 7: The Ranking Algorithm (BM25)
*   **Why BM25?** It's the industry standard (used by Elasticsearch/Lucene).
*   **Mathematical Advantage:**
    *   **Term Frequency Saturation:** Prevents word repetition from skewing results.
    *   **Length Normalization:** Adjusts scores based on document length.
*   **Formula Focus:** Discusses $k1$ and $b$ parameters used for tuning.

## Slide 9: Evaluation & Quality Metrics
*   **How we measure success:**
    *   **Precision:** How many results are actually relevant.
    *   **Recall:** How many relevant items we captured from the total.
    *   **F1-Score:** The perfect balance between the two.
*   **Result:** High performance across diverse tech-related queries.
